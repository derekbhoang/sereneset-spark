# SereneSet Launch System

## Voice

- Calm, precise, and useful.
- Prefer one clear promise to several soft claims.
- Describe an easier routine without clinical or therapeutic language.

## Visual Direction

- Keep the product stack central and fully visible.
- Use charcoal, soft white, mint, violet, and coral as a balanced palette.
- Preserve generous negative space and strong left-aligned typography.
- Avoid spa cliches, botanical decoration, and unsupported product claims.

## Required Copy

- Product name: SereneSet Essentials Kit
- Primary idea: One calm decision at a time.
- Primary action: Discover the kit

## Channel Notes

- Paid social: keep essential copy inside the center-safe area.
- Video: show the product in the first two seconds and use restrained motion.
- Email: use the launch line as a headline, never as body copy.
